Earth ponies

Earth ponies possess a diverse range of talents and capabilities. One recurring trait, however, is their physical prowess. Physically very powerful, some Earth ponies are capable of performing incredible feats of strength with little or no visible strain and enjoying long-lasting stamina as well.

Pegasi

Pegasi demonstrate great speed and agility in the air. They can walk, sit, and lie on clouds, while the other ponies need magic in order to do so. Their wings are strong and prehensile, as they can use them as extra limbs to perform tasks that only winged creatures can do.

Unicorns

Unicorns have a long horn protruding from their forehead which glows when their magical powers are used. When unicorns cast magic, it produces a colored aura around their horns and any objects their magic affects. The color of the glow is different among individuals, usually matching the color of their eyes or a prominent color of their cutie mark.

Alicorns

Alicorns are ponies with a larger body than most other pony races in Equestria. They have the strength of an earth pony, the wings of a pegasus, and the magic of a unicorn. They are far stronger, can fly faster, and can cast more powerful spells than other ponies. They are the rulers of Equestria and can usually live for a very long period of time.

Dragons in Equestria resemble classical European dragons, with large bodies, long necks and tails, scales, and bat-like wings. They love to eat gems and can breathe fire. Dragons come in various shapes and sizes. Some of them are short and are only two to three times the height of an average pony, while others are large and towers over most creatures in Equestria.

Griffons resemble the griffons in classical fantasy tales. Their front half resembles an eagle, while the back half resembles a lion. Griffons are depicted as greedy creatures whose lust for gold and treasure is stated to rival that of dragons, and they only offer their services in exchange for bits. Only a few of them are kind towards others.

Griffons reside in Griffonstone, which used to be a great a majestic kingdom under the rule of King Grover. However, the city became a small, shabby-looking town when the griffon's treasure was stolen away by a monster. The griffons returned to their selfish ways upon the decline of their kingdom.

Changelings are a race of creatures that are equine in appearance but possess insect-like characteristics. They first appeared as creatures with black carapace-like fur-less exteriors, webbed manes and tails, blue reflection-less eyes, fangs, bent horns, jagged ears, insect-like wings, and holes in their legs. Following the metamorphosis they have underwent in the season 6 finale, they now have varied coat colors and insect characteristics, and they no longer have fangs or holes in their bodies. They also have more physical differences among them: some possess longer, more jagged horns, and others no longer have horns at all. Some also have a trio of gemstone-like objects in their chests.

Yaks are large, powerful bovine creatures with large horns and are covered with thick fur and mane all over their bodies. They are known for their strength and often smash objects to display their power towards other creatures, especially during the holiday Yickslurbertfest. They live in Yakyakistan, a snowy region north of the Crystal Empire.

Hippogriffs are creatures that are a cross between a pony and an eagle. They reside in the Kingdom of Hippogriffia, made up of a small cluster of dormant volcanic islands situated far to the south of Equestria. Mount Aris is the heart of the Kingdom of Hippogriffia, completely dominating the surrounding area due to its incredible height. Kirins are pony-like creatures that have scales covering their back and the middle of their heads. They have large, curved horns on their foreheads, which they use to cast spells. They have cloven hooves, leonine tails, and a large, dorsal mane covering the side of their heads. Their furs, manes, horns, and scales are colorful, just like the ponies in Equestria. Kirins also have the ability to turn into niriks when they become angry. Niriks have black fur and have flames sprouting around their heads, tail, and feet. Their eyes become completely white, and they have fangs in their mouths.

Kirins live in Peaks of Peril, a remote mountain range covered with grass, trees, and other lush plants.
